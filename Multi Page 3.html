Why You Should Watch

In "Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse," Miles Morales embarks on a new adventure as he navigates the multiverse, encountering various Spider-People from different dimensions. After a tumultuous confrontation with a powerful new villain, Miles meets Gwen Stacy, who introduces him to the Spider-Society, a group of Spider-People dedicated to protecting the multiverse. However, tensions arise when Miles's unique approach to heroism clashes with the Society's strict rules, particularly regarding the preservation of canon events in each Spider-Person's life. As he grapples with his identity and the responsibilities of being Spider-Man, Miles must confront his place within this vast network of heroes. The film explores themes of belonging, sacrifice, and the complexity of heroism, culminating in a climactic showdown that tests Miles's resolve and sets the stage for future adventures.
